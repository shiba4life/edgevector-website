# Formation Filing Checklist

**Total estimated cost: ~$505-890 (filing fees only, no attorney)**

---

## Phase 1: Edge Vector Foundation (Non-Profit) — $305-630

### Formation
- [ ] File Articles of Incorporation with WA Secretary of State ($30, online at sos.wa.gov/corps)
- [ ] Adopt Bylaws
- [ ] Adopt Conflict of Interest Policy
- [ ] Hold organizational meeting and record minutes
- [ ] Apply for EIN (free, instant, irs.gov)
- [ ] Open bank account

### Tax-Exempt Status
- [ ] File Form 1023-EZ ($275) or 1023 ($600) with IRS
- [ ] Register with WA Secretary of State Charities Program (required for soliciting donations)
- [ ] File for WA state tax exemption (Department of Revenue)

---

## Phase 2: Edge Vector Labs LLC (For-Profit) — $200-260

**Prerequisites: Foundation must have EIN and bank account first.**

### Formation
- [ ] File Articles of Organization with WA Secretary of State ($200, online at sos.wa.gov/corps)
- [ ] Adopt Operating Agreement (signed by Foundation as sole member)
- [ ] Apply for EIN (free, instant, irs.gov) — separate EIN from Foundation
- [ ] Open bank account (separate from Foundation account)
- [ ] Register with WA Department of Revenue (for B&O tax)

### Profit Sharing
- [ ] Execute Profit Sharing Unit Agreements for Tom and Fei
- [ ] Execute IP Assignment Agreements for Tom and Fei
- [ ] No 83(b) election needed — PSUs are not equity

---

## Ongoing (Both Entities)

### Foundation
- [ ] File Form 990 annually with IRS
- [ ] File Form 990-T for UBTI from LLC operations
- [ ] Disclose LLC as related entity on Form 990
- [ ] WA Charities renewal (annually)

### LLC
- [ ] WA Annual Report ($60/year, due on anniversary of formation)
- [ ] WA B&O tax (0.471% for services; first $100k/year exempt)
- [ ] Federal tax — single-member LLC is disregarded entity; income on Foundation's Form 990-T as UBTI
- [ ] Quarterly profit sharing payments within 45 days of quarter end
- [ ] Issue 1099-NEC or W-2 for profit sharing payments
- [ ] Maintain corporate minutes for all Member/Manager actions

### Cross-Entity
- [ ] Keep Foundation and LLC finances strictly separate
- [ ] Document all transactions between entities at arm's length
- [ ] Foundation Board must approve major LLC decisions per Operating Agreement
